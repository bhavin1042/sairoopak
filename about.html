Who We Are

Sairoopak Music Academy!

Firstly, I seek blessings of my Guru and Swami Bhagwan Shree Satya Sai Baba. He has blessed me right from my childhood for my musical career. If I tell about my own career, the spark music was ignited by my beloved Papa Shree Sureshchandra and my loving Mom Shrimati Hemalataben.

I believe I am no where without them because they have been my Gurus and admirers. Next to my parents' inevitable support, my Maternal Uncle-Aunt i.e. Shree Santoshbhai and Shrimati Hemalben Vaidya as well as Upamaben Vaidya have played a pivotal role in the journey of music for me.

Our Journey

2023

The possessed knowledge has helped me to run Sai Roopak Music academy since 9th June 2003. Luckily, more than 1000 students have passed through my training of the Vocal, the Harmonium, the Guitar and last but not the least the Tabla.

Learning Never Stops. "Guru Shishya Parampara" is a life lasting process and I shall continue it forever. Along with the pursuit of learning the Tabla, the Piano and the Guitar, I have also been pursuing "Alankar" in the field of Vocal i.e. the Masters of Music under the light of Shree Dilip Vispute's torch of knowledge.

2009

Simultaneously, God has also given me an opportunity to learn the Piano that too from Shree Johnny Fernandez. He has been showering his knowledge of the Piano from 2009.

2007

The field of Music is like an ocean, the deeper you dive, the more pearls you get. I am very passionate about music so I want to learn, my eagerness to know more and more has inspired me to meet the Arranger and the Composer Shree Shyam Rajput. Shyamji Sir has been coaching me the Guiter for a decade now since 2007.

After the unexpected death of Shree Ustad Sharafat Ali Khan, presently I have been gathering the knowledge of Tabla from Shrimati Panditayan Anuradha Pal. I feel honoured to become Ma'am's disciple.

2003

After the first break throught of becoming Visharad, I have got the privilege to become the disciple of Late Shree Ustad Sharafat Ali Khan (the father of famous Music Director Sajid- Wajid). I have learnt the Tabla from him for 10 long years starting from 2003 to 2013.

My formal training started under Shree Pravinbhai Patel (Bilimora) and Shree Harsheshbhai Mehta (Bilimora).These two have taught me the ABCD of the Vocal, the Harmonium and the Tabla. By the Grace of God and the able guidance of Shree Pravinbhai and Harsheshbhai, I have achieved the Degree of Visharad (Bacheler of Music).
